ICT1512 Pass Mark Calculator

ICT15M1 is a non-venue based continuous assessment module. Your year mark contributes 100% towards your final mark and you need to obtain a subminimum of 50% in order to pass:
[Assignment 1 * 0.16] + [Assignment 2 * 0.17] + [Assignment 3 * 0.17] + [Assignment 4 * 0.5] = Year
All seven (7) of the assignment results contributes towards your year mark, which in turn is your final mark.

All marks (input) is supposed to be percentages you got in assignments.

Calculating your final mark

[Ass1 * 0.07] + [Ass2 * 0.10] +[Ass3 * 0.07] + [Ass4 * 0.10]+[Ass5 * 0.06] + [Ass6 * 0.10]+[Ass7 * 0.5] = Final mark

  • The final mark must be 50% or higher in order for you to be declared competent (Passed).
  • There are NO supplementary examination
